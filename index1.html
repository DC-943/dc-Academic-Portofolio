About Me

Dr Jinging Meng joined the School of Infrastructure Engineering, Nanchang University, China, in Jan. 2024 as a full Professor. He is a member of the International Society of Rock Mechanics and a member of the Swedish Geotechnical Engineers Society .

He has hosted and completed one research project funded by the Swedish Transport Administration, co-hosted one project funded by the Australian Research Council, and was selected by the Overseas Young Talent Program of China National Natural Science Foundation in 2023. He has achieved a series of research results in computational rock mechanics, contact dynamics discrete element theory and slope stability analysis, and has published over 40 high-quality academic papers in top computational mechanics journals and international rock mechanics journals, including the "International Journal for Numerical Methods in Engineering," "Computers and Geotechnics" and "Acta Geotechnica."

He serves as the academic editor including the "International Journal for Numerical Methods in Engineering," "Computers and Geotechnics" and "International Journal of Rock Mechanics and Mining Sciences." He is also a reviewer for over 20 international journals in geotechnical engineering and computational rock mechanics.
