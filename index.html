About Me

Dr Jinging Meng joined the School of Infrastructure Engineering, Nanchang University, China, in Jan. 2024 as a full Professor. He is a member of the International Society of Rock Mechanics and a member of the Swedish Geotechnical Engineers Society .

He serves as the academic editor-in-chief of the international journal "Journal of Rock Mechanics and Geotechnical Engineering," a youth editorial board member of "Deep Underground Science and Engineering," a guest editor of "Applied Rheology," and a guest editor for "Journal of Rock Mechanics and Geotechnical Engineering." He is also a reviewer for over 20 international journals in geotechnical engineering and computational rock mechanics.

He has hosted and completed one research project funded by the Swedish Transport Administration, co-hosted one project funded by the Australian Research Council , and was selected by the National Natural Science Foundation of China's the overseas young talent program in 2023. He has achieved a series of research results in computational rock mechanics, contact dynamics discrete element theory and slope stability analysis. He has published over 40 high-quality academic papers in top computational mechanics journals and international rock mechanics journals, including the "International Journal for Numerical Methods in Engineering," "Computers and Geotechnics" and "Acta Geotechnica."
